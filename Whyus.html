WHY US?

 

Why  SunMoon ?


At SunMoon, we equip you with everything you need to succeed. This means more than just quality accommodation in Swansea. It means dedicated study spaces, 24/7 service, friendly staff and a buzzing social calendar packed with plenty of unmissable events and experiences.
PreviousPrevious

IN THE ROOM

We offer a choice of different room types, from ensuite rooms and shared studios to spacious mezzanine apartments, all of which are beautifully designed by our interior design team. All room types include a desk, a study chair, a flat screen TV and a double wardrobe offering plenty of storage space.

DEDICATED TEAM

Our friendly team of staff are dedicated to making your time with us as simple and straightforward as possible. With up to eleven people working at each development, our experienced teams span concierge and front of house staff, maintenance workers, bar staff and security guards, who are all on hand to help you make the most of your time with us.

What's include?


We want to make sure you’re happy, confident and comfortable while studying. That’s why all of our developments include dedicated services, study spaces and communal areas, bringing people together for joint study, casual catch ups and a range of socials and events. In our beautifully designed rooms and apartments, you’ll benefit from flat-screen TVs, Wi-Fi and plenty of storage space.
