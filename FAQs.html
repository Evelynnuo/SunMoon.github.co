FAQs

Are there any upfront fees like a deposit?

We take a booking fee of £100 when you decide to book a property with us. This is allocated to your rent. That’s it! There’s no big deposit to find and no cheeky agency fees, you’ll only pay us rent. Please note, if you book a property through a letting agent and not directly with us they may charge their own fees.

What is an guarantor? Do I need a guarantor?

The guarantor is the co-signer of the lease, usually the family of the lessee. If the lessee cannot perform the lease, the guarantor is obliged to pay the rent for the contractor. If the tenant cannot prove that they have enough funds or income to pay for the rent, many apartments will ask the tenant to provide a guarantor.

May I cancel my reservation?

For the specific cancellation policy of an apartment, please contact our reservation counselor to inquire.

What happens if something goes wrong in my property?

We have a highly reactive maintenance team to get those little things sorted as quickly as possible. All you have to do is fill out the form on our website and someone from the maintenance team will get back to you within 24 hours. One of our certified tradesmen will have your place back to perfect condition in no time at all. We've also got an emergency call out team on standby 24/7.

What happens if you no longer want to live at the property / drop out of university?

All tenants have signed a SunMoon Agreement. This is a legally binding document with a fixed term of between 44 – 52 weeks. As such you are legally bound to pay all rent due during this time regardless of your student status. However, you may replace yourself in the tenancy, absolving you of future payments. Please contact Condor Properties for further information on this.
